At Utilis Technologies, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Who We Are

Utilis Technologies is an IT consulting firm based in the UK. We provide cloud infrastructure, security, and IT management services to businesses. You can contact us at dean@utilistech.co.uk.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect data through:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email, company, and inquiry details.
  • Calendly Bookings: Name, email, and meeting preferences.
  • Website Analytics: Browsing behavior and device information via Google Analytics.
  • Direct Communication: Any information you provide via email or meetings.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Schedule and conduct consultations
  • Send relevant service updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of:

  • Consent: When you fill out a form or contact us.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services and website functionality.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with UK and EU laws.
  • Contract: When you engage us for services.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary:

  • Contact Inquiries: 2 years or until contract completion.
  • Analytics Data: 26 months (Google Analytics default).
  • Client Data: Duration of contract + 7 years for accounting/audit purposes.

6. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Calendly, Formspree, Google Analytics (data processors).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • Team Members: Only on a need-to-know basis.

7. Use of AI Tools

We may use AI tools for data analysis and content improvement. Your data is processed in compliance with UK GDPR. We do not use your personal data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Request we correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request we delete your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Object: Opt out of certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us at dean@utilistech.co.uk with "Data Rights Request" in the subject line.

9. Cookies

Our website uses:

  • Google Analytics: To understand visitor behavior (anonymous).
  • Session Cookies: To maintain your session on our site.
  • Calendly Cookies: To support booking functionality.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

10. External Links

Our website contains links to third-party sites (Calendly, LinkedIn, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before using their services.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
